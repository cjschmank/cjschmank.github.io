Biography

Christopher J. Schmank is a PhD graduate student at Claremont Graduate University studying under the supervision of Andrew R.A. Conway, PhD. His research interests include the impact of psychosocial stress on language production and general cognitive abilities as well as statistical modeling techniques like latent variable modeling and psychometric network analysis.
